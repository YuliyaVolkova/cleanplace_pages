Steam cleaning of upholstered furniture

What is inside your furniture?

  • Pathogenic bacteria
  • Harmful fungi
  • Ingrained stains
Upholstered furniture and mattesses like other interior items used for years constantly accumulates dust, wool, dust mites, bacteria and dirt. It harms indoor microclimate, hurts your health, exacerbates chronic diseases and delivers many problems to allergic people and people suffering from respiratory problems. Steam cleaning of upholstered furniture will help to disinfect furniture without using corrosive chemicals. Deep cleaning allows you to get rid of dust, bacteria, foul odors.
